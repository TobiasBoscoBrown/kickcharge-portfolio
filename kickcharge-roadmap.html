MyGuy Plumbing & Drain

Plumbing · Toms River / Wall Township, NJ

Proven result: Monthly revenue grew from $38,808 to $334,026 over 5 years. Cost per lead dropped from $73.57 to $8.56. GBP now drives 67% of call volume.

  • Double down on GBP for every location with weekly posts, photo uploads, Q&A, and service menus.
  • Build hyperlocal SEO with neighborhood-specific pages and Nearby Now check-ins.
  • Integrate ServiceTitan + CallRail attribution so every organic call is tracked to source and keyword.
  • Maintain a long-term content calendar for seasonal plumbing issues to capture year-round search.
