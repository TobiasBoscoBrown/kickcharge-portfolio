KickCharge SEO Roadmap

Here's Exactly What I'd Do

I studied KickCharge's model and the verticals you serve. This is my strategic playbook for turning every contractor account into a consistent organic growth engine.

SEO Department Foundation
Applies to every KickCharge account · Technical + Local + Content
My Assessment
The best SEO program for a multi-account agency is one that scales without losing quality. I would establish a standard operating framework that every account follows, then customize by vertical, service area, and competitive intensity.
01 Standardized Account Onboarding Foundation
  • Technical baseline audit covering crawlability, indexation, schema, page speed, mobile usability, and Core Web Vitals.
  • GBP audit and optimization for every location, including categories, service attributes, photos, posts, and Q&A.
  • Keyword universe map by service + location + intent, imported into a shared tracking system.
  • Competitor benchmark to set realistic rank, traffic, and conversion targets for each account.
  • Reporting dashboard in GA4 / GSC / Looker Studio with monthly KPIs visible to client and internal teams.
02 Quality & Process Discipline Foundation
  • Monthly audit cadence for every account, not just the problem accounts.
  • QA checklist before any page, GBP update, or report leaves the department.
  • Documentation library for technical fixes, content templates, and client communication.
  • Cross-team handoff protocol with Web, Paid Ads, and Operations so SEO is never an afterthought.
  • Algorithm update response plan to assess impact quickly and communicate action to clients.
HVAC SEO Playbook
High seasonal demand · Emergency + replacement + maintenance intent
My Assessment
HVAC search is dominated by urgent, high-intent queries. The winners are the companies that rank in the local pack, answer the question immediately, and make booking a call frictionless.
01 Local SEO & GBP Highest Impact
  • Emergency service keywords - "AC repair near me", "furnace not working", "24 hour HVAC" - mapped to city pages and GBP posts.
  • Seasonal post cadence - pre-summer tune-ups, winter furnace checks, filter reminders.
  • Service-area targeting - dedicated pages for every city and neighborhood served, not just the headquarters city.
  • Review velocity system - automated follow-up to capture reviews immediately after service calls.
02 Content That Converts High Impact
  • Repair vs replace guides - "How long does an AC unit last?" captures replacement-ready homeowners.
  • Cost and pricing content - transparent pages for tune-up costs, replacement estimates, and financing.
  • FAQ schema on every service page so Google surfaces answers directly in search.
  • Maintenance plan landing pages to convert recurring-revenue customers with lower acquisition cost.
Plumbing SEO Playbook
Emergency-heavy · Location-specific · Trust-driven
My Assessment
Plumbing customers are often in crisis. They pick the company that shows up first, looks trustworthy, and answers the phone. SEO has to win the local pack and remove every doubt before they click.
01 Local Pack Dominance Highest Impact
  • GBP optimization for emergency, commercial, and residential service categories.
  • City-specific landing pages for "plumber in [city]", "water heater repair [city]", "drain cleaning [city]".
  • Citation cleanup across top 50 directories to ensure NAP consistency and authority signals.
  • Review response system that turns reviews into fresh, trust-building content on the profile.
02 Trust & Conversion Content High Impact
  • Transparent pricing pages for common jobs: clogged drain, water heater replacement, leak repair.
  • License, insurance, and warranty prominently displayed on every service page.
  • Before/after project pages with photos and location tags to build local relevance.
  • Click-to-call and scheduling above the fold on every page, especially mobile.
Roofing SEO Playbook
High-ticket · Insurance-driven · Storm-event opportunity
My Assessment
Roofing is a high-consideration, high-ticket purchase. Customers research materials, insurance claims, and contractor reputation before they call. SEO needs to capture both emergency storm traffic and long-cycle replacement research.
01 Storm & Emergency SEO Highest Impact
  • Storm-damage landing pages prepared and published before hail/wind season peaks.
  • Insurance claim content - "Does insurance cover roof replacement?" is a top-of-funnel winner.
  • Local service pages for every metro area and suburb with real project examples.
  • GBP storm alerts - posts and updates during weather events to capture surge search.
02 Authority Content High Impact
  • Material comparison guides - asphalt vs metal vs tile, lifespan, cost, and climate fit.
  • Case study pages by city with before/after photos and customer testimonials.
  • FAQ and schema addressing permits, warranties, timelines, and financing.
  • Review generation focused on high-value replacement jobs to build trust signals.
Electrical SEO Playbook
Safety + emergency + commercial · Code-aware trust
My Assessment
Electrical customers prioritize safety, speed, and credentials. SEO should emphasize licensed expertise, emergency availability, and clear service definitions for both residential and commercial buyers.
01 Service & Trust Signals Highest Impact
  • Licensed electrician pages - city-specific pages with license numbers, insurance, and safety protocols.
  • Emergency service SEO - "power outage electrician", "breaker panel sparking", "24/7 electrical repair".
  • Commercial service pages - separate funnels for B2B construction, tenant improvement, and maintenance.
  • GBP service menus with every electrical service listed and linked to the matching landing page.
02 Educational Content Steady Growth
  • Code and safety guides - "When do you need a permit for electrical work?"
  • Panel upgrade content - one of the highest-value residential jobs, with strong search intent.
  • EV charger installation pages - fast-growing query space with premium margins.
  • Video content for YouTube SEO showing real electricians explaining common issues.
Garage Door SEO Playbook
Urgent repair + install · Visual + trust-driven
My Assessment
Garage door issues stop daily life. Customers search for fast, local repair. Winning means local pack visibility, strong reviews, and clear proof that the company can handle both broken springs and full replacements.
01 Local + Emergency SEO Highest Impact
  • Same-day repair pages - "garage door repair near me", "broken spring repair [city]".
  • City landing pages for every service area with unique project photos and local copy.
  • Review-driven local pack strategy with targeted review requests after completed jobs.
  • Click-to-call prominence on mobile, since most garage door searches happen on a phone.
02 Product & Install Content Steady Growth
  • Door style galleries - organized by material, insulation, and design to capture visual shoppers.
  • Opener comparison pages - belt vs chain vs smart opener, with pros, cons, and pricing.
  • Maintenance content - "how to lubricate garage door tracks" captures DIYers who later call for service.
  • Before/after project pages to showcase transformation and build local trust.
